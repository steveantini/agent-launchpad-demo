BEST PRACTICES

"Human in the loop" - AI cannot replace your legal advice and judgment. Use agents as an assistant to augment your work.

Version Control - To ensure your agent's output is based on the most accurate information, you are responsible for uploading the latest approved versions to the 'Knowledge' section of the watsonx Orchestrate agent. This is required until a central 'single source of truth' repository is established.
